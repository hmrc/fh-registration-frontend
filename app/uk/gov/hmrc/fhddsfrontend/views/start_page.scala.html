Start of journey

Use this service to:

  • apply to register your fulfilment business for the Fulfilment House Due Diligence Scheme (FHDDS)
  • amend an existing application

It normally takes around 5 minutes to complete.

Make sure you've read the FHDDS guidance before you apply.

Start business partnership
Start Ltd company

Before you start

To use this service, you'll need:

  • a valid Government Gateway account (you can sign up for one on the next page)
  • your Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR)

You may also need:

  • your company's trading name (if you have one)
  • the addresses of your trading premises
  • the names and National Insurance numbers of all business directors and company secretaries

Other ways to apply

You can contact HMRC if you want to apply online but can't use a computer without help.
